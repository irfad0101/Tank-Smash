Game Objectives

Tank Smash is a multiplayer player game where at most 5 players can play at the same time. Objective of each player is to collect more and more points and coins while surviving throughout the gameplay. In the world of Tank Smash you are on your own. Although you can form alliances, they are temporary. Ultimately you are on your own. It's your responsibility to survive until the end. It's each against each other.

Game World

The game world is 10x10 grid.It mainly contains follwing objects

  1. Brick Walls.

  2. Stones

  3. Water Pit
