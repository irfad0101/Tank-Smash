Programming Challenge II

This game was developed under the module Programming Challenge II, offered to semester 4 undergraduates by Department of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Mortuwa. We were given the server side of the game and our task was to develop the client side.

AI

Tank Smash game client's AI consider following scenarios in the given order when deciding the next move.

  • If the health is below 100, search and move towards the nearest life pack using Breadth-first search algorithm.
  • If an enemy tank is in range, shoot if it can be directly shot or turn to the direction where the tank is.
  • Search and move towards nearest coin pack.
  • Shoot at a brick in range or move towards nearest brick.
